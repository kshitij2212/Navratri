1.Shailaputri

Daughter of the Mountain King Himavat (The Himalayas)

Avatar of Durga or Parvati.

Mantra: ॐ देवी शैलपुत्र्यै नमः॥ वंदे वाद्द्रिछतलाभाय चंद्रार्धकृतशेखराम| वृषारूढां शूलधरां शैलपुत्री यशस्विनीम् || या देवी सर्वभू ;तेषु माँ शैलपुत्री रूपेण संस्थिता। नमस्तस्यै नमस्तस्यै नमस्तस्यै नमो नमः||

Himavan (father), Menavati (mother)

2.Maa Brahmacharini

Brahmacharini (Sanskrit: ब्रह्मचारिणी) means a devoted female student who lives in an Ashrama with her Guru along with other students.

Mantra: दधाना करपद्माभ्यामक्षमालाकमण्डलू। देवी प्रसीदतु मयि ब्रह्मचारिण्यनुत्तमा॥2

Symbols: Jaap mala, kamandalu, lotus and rudraksha mala

3.Devi Chandraghanta

Chandraghanta is the “Shakti” of Lord Shiva in the form of Chandrashekhara.

Mantra: पिण्डजप्रवरारुढा चण्डकोपास्त्रकैर्युता। प्रसादं तनुते मह्यं चन्द्रघण्टेति विश्रुता॥

Weapon: Trishul, Lotus, Gada, Kamandal, Sword, Bow, Arrow, Japa maala, Abhayamudra, Gyan mudra

4.Maa Kushmanda

Goddess Kushmanda has eight hands and is thus also known as Ashtabhuja Devi.

Mantra: सुरासम्पूर्णकलशं रुधिराप्लुतमेव च। दधाना हस्तपद्माभ्यां कूष्माण्डा शुभदास्तु मे॥

Weapons: Lotus, Chakra, Kamandalu, Dhanusha (Bow), Arrow, Gada (Mace), Akshamala (Rosary), Jar of holy Elixir i.e. Sura and blood

5.Maa Skandamata

Her name comes from Skanda, an alternate name for the war god Kartikeya, and Mata,meaning mother.

Mantra: सिंहासनगता नित्यं पद्माश्रितकरद्वया। शुभदास्तु सदा देवी स्कन्दमाता यशस्विनी॥

She is regularly known as "The Goddess of Fire".

6.Katyayani

She is an aspect of Mahadevi and the slayer of the tyrannical demon Mahishasura.

Mantra: चंद्रहासोज्जवलकरा शार्दूलवरवाहना। कात्यायनी शुभं दध्यादेवी दानवघातिनि।।

Weapons: Khadga (longsword), Padma or Lotus, Abhayamudra, Varadamudra

7.Kalratri

She is first referenced in the Devi Mahatmya. Kalaratri is one of the fearsome forms of the goddess.

Mantra: एकवेणी जपकर्णपुरा नग्न कालरात्री भीषणा| दंष्ट्रकराल्वदनं घोरं मुक्तकेश्वरम्|| ललजतक्षं लंबोष्टिं शतकर्णं तथैव च| वामपादोल्लासोल्लोह लतकन्तकभूशणम्||

Weapons: Hooked vajra and curved sword, Abhayamudra, Varadamudra

8.Mahagauri

The name Mahagauri translates to extremely bright, clean complexioned, with a shine like the moon. (Mahā, महा = great; Gauri, गौरी = bright, clean).

Mantra: श्वेते वृषे समारुढा श्वेताम्बरधरा शुचिः। महागौरी शुभं दघान्महादेवप्रमोददा॥

Weapons: Trident, Damaru, Abhayamudra, Varada mudra

9.Siddhidatri

The meaning of her name is as follows: Siddhi means supernatural power or meditative ability, and Dhatri means giver or bestower.

Mantra: सिद्धगन्धर्वयक्षाघैरसुरैरमरैरपि। सेव्यमाना सदा भूयात् सिद्धिदा सिद्धिदायिनी॥ वन्दे वांछित मनोरथार्थ चन्द्रार्घकृत शेखराम्। कमलस्थितां चतुर्भुजा सिद्धीदात्री यशस्वनीम्॥ स्वर्णावर्णा निर्वाणचक्रस्थितां नवम् दुर्गा त्रिनेत्राम्। शख, चक्र, गदा, पदम, धरां सिद्धीदात्री भजेम्॥

Weapons: Mace, Chakra, Shankha, lotus in which 8 siddhis are absorbed
